Balaji Fast Food Dashboard

Overview
This dashboard was created to analyze the sales performance of fast food items,
transaction patterns, and payment preferences using interactive visuals in Power BI.

Data Source :
Kaggle

Note: The result shows that the dataset contains multiple transaction records with 11 columns. The columns include order details (order_id, date, item_name, item_type, item_price, quantity), transaction information (transaction_amount, transaction_type, received_by, time_of_sale), and calculated revenue. Most columns have complete values, but the transaction_type column contains some missing (Null) entries, as seen in the first row where the payment method is not specified.

3. Data Visualization with Power BI

A. DASHBOARD

B. COUNT TRANSACTION BY TYPE

Note: Based on the Count Transaction By Type visualization, cash transactions remain the most common payment method, accounting for 47.6% (476 transactions). Online payments follow closely with 41.7% (417 transactions), while unkown payment methods make up the smallest share at 10.7% (107 transactions).

C. REVENUE BY ITEM NAME

Note: The Revenue By Item Name chart shows that Sandwich is the top-performing product, generating the highest revenue at over 60K. Frankie and Cold Coffee follow closely, both contributing significantly to total sales. Meanwhile, Sugarcane Juice, Panipuri, and Aalopuri generate lower revenue in comparison, indicating a smaller share in the overall product performance.

D. COUNT TRANSACTION BY TIME

Note: The Count Transaction By Time chart indicates that transactions are relatively evenly distributed across different times of the day, with Afternoon and Night slightly leading in volume. Evening and Midnight follow closely, while Morning has the lowest, yet still significant, number of transactions.

E. REVENUE BY MONTH

Note:
  • May 2022, October 2022, and January 2023 show the highest revenue points, each reaching close to or above 28K.
  • June 2022 marks the lowest revenue period, dropping below 18K.
  • Revenue shows a cyclical pattern with noticeable ups and downs rather than consistent growth.
  • From January to March 2023, revenue remains relatively stable but slightly lower than the January peak.

F. TOTAL REVENUE

Note: The business achieved a total revenue of 275K, reflecting strong overall sales performance. A total of 8,162 products were sold during the analyzed period, indicating consistent customer demand and a healthy sales volume. These figures highlight both the market reach and the effectiveness of sales strategies in driving revenue and product movement.

4. Conclusion

Based on our analysis, there are some conclusions.
  • Total revenue reached 275K with 8,162 products sold, showing solid sales performance.
  • Cash transactions (47.6%) slightly outperformed online transactions (41.7%), with other payment methods making up 10.7%.
  • Sandwich generated the highest revenue, while Cold coffee had the highest quantity sold.
  • Sales were distributed relatively evenly across times of the day, with slight peaks in Afternoon and Night.
  • Monthly revenue trends showed fluctuations, with notable peaks in May 2022, October 2022, and January 2023.

5. Recommendation

Based on our analysis, there are some conclusions.
  • Promote top-selling products like Sandwich and Cold coffee while exploring ways to boost lower-performing items.
  • Strengthen online sales channels to close the gap with cash transactions.
  • Launch time-based promotions during off-peak hours to balance transaction distribution.
  • Investigate factors behind revenue peaks to replicate those strategies in other months.
  • Continue monitoring product performance and transaction trends for targeted marketing efforts.
